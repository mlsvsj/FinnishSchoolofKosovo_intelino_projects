## 🚂 Project 1: Move → Wait → Move  

### 🧠 About Scratch  
Scratch is a visual programming tool where we use blocks instead of typing code.  
Each block is an instruction that tells the train what to do step by step.

### 📘 Project Description  
In this project, we control the intelino train using simple blocks.  
The train starts moving, then stops, and then moves again.

### 🧱 Code  
when movement forward  
drive forward at 30 cm/s  
pause driving for 5 seconds  
drive forward at 30 cm/s  

### 🚂 What Happens  
- The train moves forward  
- The train stops for 5 seconds  
- The train moves again  

### 🎯 Student Task  
Make the train move forward, then stop for a few seconds, and then continue moving again.  
Try changing the speed of the train and adjust how long it stops.  
You can also add another stop or make the train move faster or slower.

### ❓ Questions  
- What happens first?  
- Why does the train stop?  
- What happens if we change the time?
