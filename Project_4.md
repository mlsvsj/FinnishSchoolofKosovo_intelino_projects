
## 🚂 Project 4: Two Stations  

### 🧠 About Scratch  
Scratch can control movement and repeat actions using sequences of blocks.

---

### 📘 Project Description  
In this project, the train stops at two different points, like two stations.

---

### 🧱 Code  
```
when movement forward  
reset distance  
drive forward at 30 cm/s  
wait until distance > 50  
stop driving  
pause driving for 3 seconds  
drive forward at 30 cm/s  
wait until distance > 100  
stop driving  
pause driving for 3 seconds  
drive forward at 30 cm/s  
```

---

### 🚂 What Happens  
- The train moves  
- It stops at the first station  
- It continues moving  
- It stops at the second station  
- Then it moves again  

---

### 🎯 Student Task  
Make the train stop at two different places.  
Try changing the distance of each stop.  
You can also change the waiting time at each station.

---

### ❓ Questions  
- How many times does the train stop?  
- What happens if we change the distances?  
- Can we add a third station?
